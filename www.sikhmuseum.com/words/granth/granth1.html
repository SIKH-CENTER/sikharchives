Illuminated Guru Granth Sahib

ca. 1830-1860
Dr. Gurpal Singh Bhuller collection

Hand written Guru Granth Sahib with 714 folios produced at the height of the Sikh manuscript tradition. Affluent Sikh patrons would commission highly talented scribes to produce elaborately decorative copies of the Guru Granth Sahib.

The bride and groom at the evening reception surrounded by their immediate families. They will soon make their grand entrance into the reception hall where over 1,000 guests await them. After an introduction of family members, speeches, musical performances, a cake cutting ceremony and a first dance, the couple and guests danced the night away while enjoying non-alcoholic drinks and a delicious meal.

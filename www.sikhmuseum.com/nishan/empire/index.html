The Sikh Empire

With the advent of the Sikh Empire of Lahore under the rule of Maharaja Ranjit Singh, the Sikhs experienced a cultural and artistic renaissance. Free to practice their religion without persecution for the first time, this era represented the pinnacle of Sikh artistic expression. During this era the Nishan Sahib banner flew from every corner of a growing Empire.

Sikhs Wearing Helmets

Studying rare early paintings dating to the time of Sikh rule in Punjab, we look for evidence of Sikhs wearing helmets. Sikh chronicles as well as eyewitness accounts of travellers to the Empire of Maharaja Ranjit Singh are also examined for any evidence of Sikh wearing helmets.

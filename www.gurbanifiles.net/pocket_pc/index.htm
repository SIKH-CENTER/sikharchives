This page provides facility to download Acrobat Reader (pdf) files relating to Sri Guru Granth Sahib for use with a Pocket PC. These pdf files are optimized for use on a Pocket PC (Windows Mobile) with Acrobat Reader installed.
Except when stated otherwise, where applicable the English translation is by Sant Singh Khalsa, MD and Phonetic transliteration (Romanization) is by Kulbir S Thind, MD.

Regarding files other than pdf, for use on your PDA (Pocket PC & Palm OS devices), go to Siknet.com/s/BanisOnPDA

To start downloading a file, click with the RIGHT mouse button on the corresponding "Download" text and then selct "Save Target As ..." (or Save Link As ..)

View striking panoramic images of the Golden Temple Complex which make you feel that your are standing there right now. The latest Immersive Imaging technology has been used to create these panoramic images. Netscape 3 or Internet Explorer 3 are minimum requirements to view these panoramics. Just click on any 'P' on the map below and wait for the image to finish downloading. When it appears, click on it and drag your mouse to the left or right to change your view.


Harimandir Sahib: A Spiritual Journey
An inspiring and uplifting audiovisual celebration of the experience of visiting Sri Harimandir Sahib. This 7.5 minute movie requires the RealNetworks Real Player 5.0 to view, if you do not have it a link to their website is provided. The movie can be viewed as a streaming video by clicking on the link below which best describes your Internet connection or as a downloadable file. If while watching the streaming movie, it does not play without interruptions, download the zip file of the movie to your hard drive instead. This higher quality movie file (golden.rm) can be extracted from the zip file and then played locally from your hard drive using Real Player 5.0.

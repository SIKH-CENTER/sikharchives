They act according to their pre-ordained destiny, which no one can erase. || 3 || It is very difficult to serve the True Guru. Surrender your head; give up your selfishness. Those who have such pre-ordained destiny come to meet the True Guru. || 4 || O mind, don't cry out that you are hungry, always hungry; stop complaining.

Composition of Guru Gobind Singh
Author Name of Composition Page Rag
Guru Gobind Singh Slok 54 (attributed) pg. 1429 Raga Jaijaiwanti

Guru Gobind Singh wrote profusely but did not include his works in Sri Guru Granth Sahib, the final version which was compiled by him. The writings of Guru Gobind Singh were compiled in the Dasam Granth after his death. Only one Slok in the Sloks of Guru Tegh Bahadur is attributed to by some to Guru Gobind Singh.

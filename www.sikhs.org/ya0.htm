Guru and Sikh
by Yuktanand Singh

Home >Resources > Essays & Articles > Essay

Guru and Sikh by Dr. Yuktanand Singh is a essay which really captures the essence of what Sikhism is all about. A well written document, written in a contemporary manner, it is easy and enjoyable to read as the author really gets down to the heart of the matter. It achieves something which very few such papers ever do, presenting Sikhism clearly and concisely for both the Sikh and non-Sikh audience in modern easy to understand language. Dr. Singh has produced a document which really is a 'must read' for anyone on the Sikh spiritual path, or for those interested in learning what the Sikh path is all about.
